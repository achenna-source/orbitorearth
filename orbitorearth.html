How to read it. Orbit emits less carbon than the ground baseline exactly when the displaced grid intensity exceeds the break-even I*. The three curves are the same model under three accounting/metric choices; your operating point (grid × mass) sits relative to the one you select. Preliminary: water savings and the born-in-orbit downlink credit are not yet in the carbon break-even, and the second-order orbital-manufacturing and avoided-construction terms are set to zero. Operational carbon in orbit is taken as ~0 (continuous dawn-dusk solar). Companion to the paper “When Does AI Compute Belong in Orbit?” — the model and parameter ranges mirror params.yaml.
